Introduction

For two weeks have we focused on honing our eyes when it comes to font choice, font hierachy, how to pick a color pallet and apply it in a stylish manner. Furthermore we have worked with animation on on websites that bring them to life. Continue down the page to learn more about my process.

Fonts & UI cards

A mockup landing pages using fonts and gray scales only

In this assignment we started out with a mockup landing page and had to do two parts. First part was to implement fonts and greyscales and the second part was to improve on existing UI cards.

3.Semester - KEA

Working with Colors & Animations

A mockup landing page with using a color pallet and animations.

In this assignment we started out with a mockup landing page and had to do create a color pallet with up to 6 colors and then use it on our website trying to use the 60-30-10 rule for colors. Second we had to create and implement a dark-mode and lastly apply animation to our website.

Conclusion

These two weeks have been a great chance to work with fonts, colors and animations and the especially the first two have hit a been sorely needed for me. I’m not expert but I feel that slowly I’m beginning to think about contrasts, font-hierachys and how to call attention to CTA’s whether through contrasting colors or animated movement.
